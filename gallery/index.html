Organic Blossoms

by Ana Girlonta

In this, I was inspired to blend elements of the sea with the tranquility of nature. This was designed using dried eucalyptus leaves, shells from St Augustine Beach in Florida, and pearl beads glued on a painted canvas. Seashells and a sand dollar are arranged in a floral composition, set against a soft blue background that evokes the serenity of the ocean. The subtle use of green leaves is intended to add a touch of natural vibrancy, making this piece a simple harmonious blend of marine life and botanical beauty.
